SUBMITTANCE AGREEMENT

Astra promotes fair working conditions for workers engaged in performing work at the properties we manage. Through our Responsible Contracting Policy (the “Policy”) we support and encourage fair wages, fair benefits, training and appropriate working conditions for workers employed by our contractors (a “Responsible Contractor”) as compensated on comparable real estate projects, based upon local market factors that include the nature of the project, comparable job or trade classiﬁcation and the scope and complexity of services provided. A Responsible Contractor must comply with all jurisdictional labour laws, statutes and regulations. A Responsible Contractor pays workers fair wages and benefits as evidenced by payroll and employee records. By submitting and signing this pre-qualification form, the contractor affirms their compliance with the Policy and agrees that it may be subject to an audit to verify compliance with the Policy in the performance of work undertaken at Astra managed projects.

PRIVACY POLICY

  • We collect and use information that is necessary to administer our business and provide our clients with outstanding customer service.
  • We may require personal information for the purposes of verifying compliance with our Policy and you agree to obtain any consents which are required under privacy legislation in order to make available and disclose the information to us.
  • We will hold your personal information in confidence.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We provide prospective and former contractors/suppliers the same protection as existing contractors/suppliers with respect to the use of personal information.
  • We may disclose information about you to persons or organizations as permitted or required by law.
  • We will retain your personal information for the duration of our service relationship.
